About The Club

The Golden State Warriors are one of the most storied franchises in NBA history. Founded in 1946 as the Philadelphia Warriors, they were one of the league's original eleven teams and won the very first BAA Championship in 1947.

The franchise relocated to San Francisco in 1962 and became the Golden State Warriors in 1971. They play their home games at the Chase Center in San Francisco, California — a state-of-the-art arena that opened in 2019.

From Wilt Chamberlain's 100-point game to Rick Barry's underhand free throws to the Splash Brothers dynasty, the Warriors have produced some of basketball's most iconic moments and players.

The modern Warriors dynasty, led by Stephen Curry, Klay Thompson, and Draymond Green, redefined basketball with their revolutionary three-point shooting and motion offense. Between 2015 and 2022, they won four NBA Championships and made six NBA Finals appearances.

Stephen Curry became the NBA's all-time three-point leader in December 2021, cementing his legacy as the greatest shooter in basketball history. The Warriors' "Strength in Numbers" mantra became synonymous with team-first basketball.
